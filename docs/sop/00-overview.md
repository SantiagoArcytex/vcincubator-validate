# Selling Your App on VCI Marketplace — Start Here

Welcome. This is the complete guide to putting your app on the VCI Marketplace and getting paid for it.

You don't need to be a hardcore engineer. If you built your app with AI help, you can finish this. Where real code is involved, we give you a tool that does it for you (more on that in Stage 4).

## What you're about to do

The whole journey, in plain terms:

1. **Get set up** — create your seller account and gather a few things.
2. **Prepare your app + listing** — collect the info and images the marketplace will ask for.
3. **Connect Stripe** — so money from sales actually reaches your bank account.
4. **Connect your app to VCI** — add a small piece so your app can check "did this person pay?" This is the only part with code, and an AI assistant does it for you.
5. **Watch your dashboard** — see sales and earnings come in.

## The big idea (read this once)

When someone buys your app on the VCI Marketplace, we give them a **license code**.

They paste that code into your app. Your app asks VCI *"is this code real and paid-for?"*. VCI answers yes or no. Your app unlocks or stays locked.

That's it. You sell access, VCI handles the money and the codes, your app just asks one question.

```
  Buyer purchases on VCI  →  Buyer gets a license code
                                      │
                                      ▼
             Buyer pastes the code into YOUR app
                                      │
                                      ▼
          Your app asks VCI: "is this code valid?"
                                      │
                          ┌───────────┴───────────┐
                          ▼                       ▼
                    Yes → unlock            No → stay locked
```

## A few things to know up front

- **You keep most of the money.** VCI takes a marketplace commission on each sale. Your exact share is shown on every sale and in your dashboard — no surprises.
- **You can sell two ways:** a one-time purchase (buyer pays once, keeps access forever) or a subscription (buyer pays monthly or yearly). You choose per listing.
- **Your app stays yours.** It lives wherever it already lives. VCI doesn't host it — we just handle the buying, the codes, and the payments.
- **Codes are owned by the buyer.** A buyer who buys your app can use their code across re-installs and devices. Your app just keeps checking it's still valid.
